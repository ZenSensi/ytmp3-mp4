How to convert a YouTube video?

  1. Search on YouTube for a video you would like to download. Copy the video URL from your browser's address bar. Please note: A valid YouTube video URL should have the following format: youtube.com/watch?v=videoId.
  2. Paste the video URL into our converter above. If you want to convert the video to an MP4 file, change the format to MP4. If no format is selected, the video will be converted to an MP3 file by default. Click the "Convert" button.
  3. The conversion process will start. Depending on the length of the video, the conversion may take a few minutes. Once the conversion is successfully completed, you can download the converted file by clicking the "Download" button.
